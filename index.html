OUR MISSION

The Syrentha J. Savio Endowment (SSE) was launched by punk-rock photographer Mark Beemer in 2002
to provide financial support for underprivileged women fighting breast cancer.

The Shirts For A Cure Project (SFAC) was then created by SSE to raise awareness about breast cancer preventition.

When a band donates a shirt design to SFAC, the design becomes exlusive to SSE.

All proceeds go directly towards helping women fight their battle with breast cancer.

Take a look around our website to find a fun t-shirt or accessory for you!

All shirts are printed on 100% pre-shrunk cotton, unless otherwise stated.
